About Us

At XPRESS TYRES LTD., we have a very fundamental philosophy that guides us: we aim to provide 100% customer satisfaction by ensuring quality products and excellent services.

In Manchester, we are one of the leading suppliers of fresh tyres from major manufacturing companies around the world. We excel in tyre fitment services as well.

At our facility, we aim to serve the people who live in or around Manchester with quality products for car tyres and van tyres. We also ensure there is a fit for every client’s requirements, no matter the budget. Hence, our product range comprises premium brands, mid-range brands, as well as budget brands.

Tyres we sell

We are the authorised retailers of iconic tyre brands like Continental, Bridgestone, Goodyear, Pirelli, Michelin, and Dunlop, whereas we also deal in budget brands such as Compass, Debica, Goalstar, Minerva, Nordex, Torque, and more.

We sell almost all the models that these manufacturers have on offer in their arsenal. From standard summer and winter models to UHP and run-flat tyres, you are sure to find every type at our facility.

We also retail budget and premium tyres at special prices for our clients. Our inventory has a wide range of specialised models for SUVs, all-terrain vehicles, vans, and other heavy vehicles.

Tyre fitment services

Tyre fitting is a professional job that requires years of experience. Even a single mistake can spell risks on-road. Safety is the obvious concern; prolonged periods of snow or rain are quite common in the UK. Under such circumstances, properly fitted tyres are nothing less than mandatory.

At XPRESS TYRES LTD., our team uses the latest available technologies for a precision tyre fitment service. It will bring you further comfort to know that we also offer expert services for wheel balancing and tyre puncture repair.

Our multiple service bays are also helpful in fitting multiple cars simultaneously. Note that our service garage in Manchester used mostly automated technologies to fit tyres, leaving very little room for errors. Also, our tyre fitment services are priced competitively
